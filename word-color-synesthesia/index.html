How it works

This tool maps words to colors based on their emotional and situational qualities. You can type, paste, or speak your text using the microphone button:

  • Positive words → Bright, warm colors (yellows, light blues, greens)
  • Negative words → Dark, cool colors (grays, dark blues, purples)
  • Action words → Bold, energetic colors (reds, oranges)
  • Peaceful words → Calm, serene colors (light blues, soft greens)
  • Neutral words → Balanced, muted colors (beiges, light grays)

The gradient shows how the emotional tone flows through the text, creating a visual representation of the text's emotional journey. Click the microphone button and start speaking to see your words appear in real-time!
