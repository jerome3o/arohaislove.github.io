How it works

This tool maps words to colors based on their emotional and situational qualities:

  • Positive words → Bright, warm colors (yellows, light blues, greens)
  • Negative words → Dark, cool colors (grays, dark blues, purples)
  • Action words → Bold, energetic colors (reds, oranges)
  • Peaceful words → Calm, serene colors (light blues, soft greens)
  • Neutral words → Balanced, muted colors (beiges, light grays)

The gradient above shows how the emotional tone flows through the text, creating a visual representation of the text's emotional journey.
